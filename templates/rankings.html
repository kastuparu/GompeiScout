How Rankings are Calculated

The quantitative data rankings are calculated based on how many points the team would have scored for their alliance on average during a match. See the FRC 2023 game manual for all point values.

The auto points are calculated based on the points for game pieces scored (3 pts for low, 4 pts for mid, 6 pts for high) as well as the points for mobility (3 pts), docking (8 pts), and balancing (12 pts).

The teleop points are calculated just based on the points for game pieces scored (2 pts for low, 3 pts for mid, 5 pts for high). Note: this does not include links (5 pts each), endgame (2 pts for parked, 6 pts for docked, 10 pts for balanced), supercharged nodes (3 pts each), and penalties (5 pts for foul, 12 pts for tech foul).

These rankings help us compare teams who perform similarly in terms of autos and scoring in teleop. This is not intended to be a pick list; the qualitative data gathered should be used in conjunction with this to make a pick list.
